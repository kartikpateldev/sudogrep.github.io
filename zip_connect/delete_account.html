1. How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your Zip Connect account and associated user data, you can do so by:

  • In-App Deletion: Open Zip Connect > Go to Settings > Tap Delete Account.
  • Online Request Form: Fill out and submit the deletion request form below.
  • Email Request: Send an email to support@sudogrep.in with the subject "Delete Zip Connect Account".

2. Data Types Deleted & Retained

Data Permanently Deleted (within 30 days):

  • Profile Details (Display name, Avatar, Email address, Firebase UID)
  • Gameplay Progress (Level completion history, stars, win streaks)
  • Virtual Economy (Coins, gems balance, skin unlocks)

Data Retained:

  • Financial transaction receipts (Google Play order IDs are kept in anonymized accounting records for up to 7 years strictly for legal and tax compliance).

Note: Deleting your account is permanent. All coins and level progress will be erased.
